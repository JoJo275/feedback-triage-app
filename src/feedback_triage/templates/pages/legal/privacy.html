Privacy

Last updated: 2026-05-07.

What we collect

SignalNest is a portfolio project. The hosted instance stores only the data you give it: your account email, your workspace name, the feedback items you log, and the submissions made through your public form.

What we do not collect

  • No third-party analytics.
  • No advertising trackers.
  • No fingerprinting beyond a single first-party session cookie.

Email

Status-change notifications go out through Resend when a submitter has provided an email. Resend is the only third party that ever sees a submitter address. Submitters can opt out from any notification footer.

Data retention

Workspaces and their feedback persist until you delete them. Account deletion removes every workspace you own and every feedback row inside them. There is no soft-delete tier.

Questions: hello@signalnest.app.
